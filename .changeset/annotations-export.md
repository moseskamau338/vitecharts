Annotations + export.

- Annotations drawn over the plot: `xLine`, `yLine`, `region`, and `point` (with
  optional labels), via a new `annotations` option.
- Export API on `Chart`: `toSVG()`, `toPNG(scale)`, `toCSV()`, `toJSON()`, and
  `download(format)` for svg/png/csv/json.
